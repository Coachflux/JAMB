Variation in Mathematics (JAMB Style)

Variation shows how one quantity (dependent variable) changes with one or more other quantities (independent variables). We use the constant of variation k to write equations. Always find k first using given values, then solve for the unknown. I will teach each part very slowly and simply. Try each example on paper first, then check my steps. You will understand everything fast.

(a) Direct Variation

Very easy explanation

When y varies directly as x (y ∝ x), when x increases, y increases by the same factor.

Formula: y = kx (or y = kxⁿ for powers).

Steps: 1. Write y = kx (or with power). 2. Find k using given values. 3. Use new value to find unknown.

10 Full Examples with Step-by-Step

1. y varies directly as x. y = 15 when x = 3. Find y when x = 12.
Step 1: y = kx
Step 2: 15 = k × 3
Step 3: k = 15 ÷ 3 = 5
Step 4: y = 5x
Step 5: y = 5 × 12 = 60
Answer: 60
2. y varies directly as x². y = 8 when x = 2. Find y when x = 5.
Step 1: y = kx²
Step 2: 8 = k × 4
Step 3: k = 8 ÷ 4 = 2
Step 4: y = 2x²
Step 5: y = 2 × 25 = 50
Answer: 50
3. Volume V varies directly as temperature T. V = 300 cm³ when T = 150 K. Find V when T = 200 K.
Step 1: V = kT
Step 2: 300 = k × 150
Step 3: k = 300 ÷ 150 = 2
Step 4: V = 2T
Step 5: V = 2 × 200 = 400 cm³
Answer: 400 cm³
4. p varies directly as q². p = 18 when q = 3. Find p when q = 6.
Step 1: p = kq²
Step 2: 18 = k × 9
Step 3: k = 18 ÷ 9 = 2
Step 4: p = 2q²
Step 5: p = 2 × 36 = 72
Answer: 72
5. y varies directly as √x. y = 6 when x = 36. Find y when x = 100.
Step 1: y = k√x
Step 2: 6 = k × 6
Step 3: k = 6 ÷ 6 = 1
Step 4: y = √x
Step 5: y = √100 = 10
Answer: 10
6. Cost C varies directly as number n of books. 200 books cost ₦12,000. Find cost of 500 books.
Step 1: C = kn
Step 2: 12000 = k × 200
Step 3: k = 12000 ÷ 200 = 60
Step 4: C = 60n
Step 5: C = 60 × 500 = ₦30,000
Answer: ₦30,000
7. x varies directly as y³. x = 54 when y = 3. Find x when y = 2.
Step 1: x = ky³
Step 2: 54 = k × 27
Step 3: k = 54 ÷ 27 = 2
Step 4: x = 2y³
Step 5: x = 2 × 8 = 16
Answer: 16
8. Distance d varies directly as time t. d = 180 km in 3 hours. Find d in 5 hours.
Step 1: d = kt
Step 2: 180 = k × 3
Step 3: k = 180 ÷ 3 = 60
Step 4: d = 60t
Step 5: d = 60 × 5 = 300 km
Answer: 300 km
9. z varies directly as x² y. z = 48 when x = 2, y = 3. Find z when x = 3, y = 4.
Step 1: z = k x² y
Step 2: 48 = k × 4 × 3
Step 3: 48 = k × 12 → k = 4
Step 4: z = 4 x² y
Step 5: z = 4 × 9 × 4 = 144
Answer: 144
10. y varies directly as x⁴. y = 81 when x = 3. Find y when x = 1.
Step 1: y = k x⁴
Step 2: 81 = k × 81
Step 3: k = 1
Step 4: y = x⁴
Step 5: y = 1⁴ = 1
Answer: 1

(b) Inverse Variation

Very easy explanation

When y varies inversely as x (y ∝ 1/x), when x increases, y decreases.

Formula: y = k / x (or y = k / xⁿ)

Steps: 1. Find k = y × x (product constant). 2. Use new value to find unknown.

10 Full Examples with Step-by-Step

1. y varies inversely as x. y = 12 when x = 4. Find y when x = 6.
Step 1: y = k / x
Step 2: k = 12 × 4 = 48
Step 3: y = 48 / 6 = 8
Answer: 8
2. Time t varies inversely as number of workers w. 8 workers take 6 days. How long for 12 workers?
Step 1: t = k / w
Step 2: k = 8 × 6 = 48
Step 3: t = 48 / 12 = 4 days
Answer: 4 days
3. y varies inversely as √x. y = 10 when x = 25. Find y when x = 100.
Step 1: y = k / √x
Step 2: k = 10 × 5 = 50
Step 3: y = 50 / 10 = 5
Answer: 5
4. p varies inversely as q². p = 18 when q = 3. Find p when q = 9.
Step 1: p = k / q²
Step 2: k = 18 × 9 = 162
Step 3: p = 162 / 81 = 2
Answer: 2
5. Resistance R varies inversely as square of diameter d. R = 20 Ω when d = 2 mm. Find R when d = 4 mm.
Step 1: R = k / d²
Step 2: k = 20 × 4 = 80
Step 3: R = 80 / 16 = 5 Ω
Answer: 5 Ω
6. Journey 240 km takes 4 hours at speed v. How long at speed 80 km/h?
Step 1: t = k / v
Step 2: k = 240 (distance constant)
Step 3: t = 240 / 80 = 3 hours
Answer: 3 hours
7. y = k / x³. y = 27 when x = 2. Find y when x = 3.
Step 1: k = 27 × 8 = 216
Step 2: y = 216 / 27 = 8
Answer: 8
8. Intensity I varies inversely as square of distance d. I = 36 when d = 2 m. Find I when d = 6 m.
Step 1: I = k / d²
Step 2: k = 36 × 4 = 144
Step 3: I = 144 / 36 = 4
Answer: 4
9. Number of days d to eat food varies inversely as number of people p. 15 people eat in 8 days. How many days for 20 people?
Step 1: d = k / p
Step 2: k = 15 × 8 = 120
Step 3: d = 120 / 20 = 6 days
Answer: 6 days
10. y varies inversely as x⁴. y = 16 when x = 2. Find y when x = 1.
Step 1: k = 16 × 16 = 256
Step 2: y = 256 / 1 = 256
Answer: 256

(c) Joint Variation

Very easy explanation

y varies jointly as x and z means y ∝ x z. Formula: y = k × x × z (or y = k xᵐ zⁿ).

Steps: 1. Find k = y / (x × z × powers). 2. Use new values to find unknown.

10 Full Examples with Step-by-Step

1. z varies jointly as x and y. z = 24 when x = 3, y = 4. Find z when x = 5, y = 6.
Step 1: z = k x y
Step 2: 24 = k × 3 × 4
Step 3: 24 = k × 12 → k = 2
Step 4: z = 2 × 5 × 6 = 60
Answer: 60
2. P varies jointly as Q and R². P = 36 when Q = 3, R = 2. Find P when Q = 4, R = 3.
Step 1: P = k Q R²
Step 2: 36 = k × 3 × 4
Step 3: 36 = k × 12 → k = 3
Step 4: P = 3 × 4 × 9 = 108
Answer: 108
3. Volume V varies jointly as r² and h. V = 88 when r = 2, h = 7 (use π = 22/7). Find V when r = 3, h = 5.
Step 1: V = k r² h
Step 2: 88 = k × 4 × 7
Step 3: 88 = k × 28 → k = 88 / 28 = 22/7
Step 4: V = (22/7) × 9 × 5 = 990 / 7 ≈ 141.43
Answer: ≈141.43
4. z varies jointly as x and y². z = 36 when x = 2, y = 3. Find z when x = 3, y = 2.
Step 1: z = k x y²
Step 2: 36 = k × 2 × 9
Step 3: 36 = k × 18 → k = 2
Step 4: z = 2 × 3 × 4 = 24
Answer: 24
5. Area A of triangle varies jointly as base b and height h. A = 30 when b = 6, h = 10. Find A when b = 8, h = 15.
Step 1: A = k b h
Step 2: 30 = k × 6 × 10
Step 3: 30 = k × 60 → k = 0.5
Step 4: A = 0.5 × 8 × 15 = 60
Answer: 60
6. P varies jointly as m and u. P = 12 when m = 2, u = 3. Find P when m = 4, u = 6.
Step 1: P = k m u
Step 2: 12 = k × 2 × 3
Step 3: 12 = k × 6 → k = 2
Step 4: P = 2 × 4 × 6 = 48
Answer: 48
7. y varies jointly as x² and z. y = 80 when x = 2, z = 5. Find y when x = 3, z = 4.
Step 1: y = k x² z
Step 2: 80 = k × 4 × 5
Step 3: 80 = k × 20 → k = 4
Step 4: y = 4 × 9 × 4 = 144
Answer: 144
8. Work W varies jointly as force F and distance d. W = 120 when F = 10, d = 12. Find W when F = 15, d = 8.
Step 1: W = k F d
Step 2: 120 = k × 10 × 12
Step 3: 120 = k × 120 → k = 1
Step 4: W = 1 × 15 × 8 = 120
Answer: 120
9. z varies jointly as x and y². z = 48 when x = 4, y = 3. Find z when x = 6, y = 2.
Step 1: z = k x y²
Step 2: 48 = k × 4 × 9
Step 3: 48 = k × 36 → k = 48 / 36 = 4/3
Step 4: z = (4/3) × 6 × 4 = 32
Answer: 32
10. P varies jointly as Q³ and R. P = 108 when Q = 3, R = 4. Find P when Q = 2, R = 6.
Step 1: P = k Q³ R
Step 2: 108 = k × 27 × 4
Step 3: 108 = k × 108 → k = 1
Step 4: P = 1 × 8 × 6 = 48
Answer: 48

(d) Partial Variation

Very easy explanation

y = a + kx (a = fixed/constant part, kx = varying part)

Steps: Use two given pairs → make two equations → solve for a and k → use new value.

10 Full Examples with Step-by-Step

1. y varies partially as x. When x = 2, y = 11; when x = 5, y = 20. Find y when x = 8.
Step 1: 11 = a + 2k
Step 2: 20 = a + 5k
Step 3: Subtract → 9 = 3k → k = 3
Step 4: 11 = a + 6 → a = 5
Step 5: y = 5 + 3×8 = 29
Answer: 29
2. Cost C of hiring a bus is partly constant and partly varies directly as distance d. C = ₦800 for 100 km, ₦1,100 for 200 km. Find C for 350 km.
Step 1: 800 = a + 100k
Step 2: 1100 = a + 200k
Step 3: Subtract → 300 = 100k → k = 3
Step 4: 800 = a + 300 → a = 500
Step 5: C = 500 + 3×350 = ₦1,550
Answer: ₦1,550
3. x – 3 varies directly as y². x = 5 when y = 2; find x when y = 6.
Step 1: x – 3 = k y²
Step 2: 5 – 3 = k × 4 → 2 = 4k → k = 0.5
Step 3: x – 3 = 0.5 y²
Step 4: x – 3 = 0.5 × 36 = 18
Step 5: x = 18 + 3 = 21
Answer: 21
4. Cost C = fixed + variable per item. C = ₦450 for 10 items, ₦750 for 20 items. Find C for 30 items.
Step 1: 450 = a + 10k
Step 2: 750 = a + 20k
Step 3: Subtract → 300 = 10k → k = 30
Step 4: 450 = a + 300 → a = 150
Step 5: C = 150 + 30×30 = ₦1,050
Answer: ₦1,050
5. When t = 3, s = 19; t = 7, s = 43. s partial as t. Find s at t = 10.
Step 1: 19 = a + 3k
Step 2: 43 = a + 7k
Step 3: Subtract → 24 = 4k → k = 6
Step 4: 19 = a + 18 → a = 1
Step 5: s = 1 + 6×10 = 61
Answer: 61
6. x = a + k y². x = 7 when y = 2; x = 19 when y = 4. Find x when y = 5.
Step 1: 7 = a + 4k
Step 2: 19 = a + 16k
Step 3: Subtract → 12 = 12k → k = 1
Step 4: 7 = a + 4 → a = 3
Step 5: x = 3 + 1×25 = 28
Answer: 28
7. Electricity bill B is partly fixed and partly varies as units u. B = ₦1,200 for 80 units, ₦1,800 for 140 units. Find B for 200 units.
Step 1: 1200 = a + 80k
Step 2: 1800 = a + 140k
Step 3: Subtract → 600 = 60k → k = 10
Step 4: 1200 = a + 800 → a = 400
Step 5: B = 400 + 10×200 = ₦2,400
Answer: ₦2,400
8. y = a + k √x. y = 10 when x = 4; y = 16 when x = 25. Find y when x = 36.
Step 1: 10 = a + k×2
Step 2: 16 = a + k×5
Step 3: Subtract → 6 = 3k → k = 2
Step 4: 10 = a + 4 → a = 6
Step 5: y = 6 + 2×6 = 18
Answer: 18
9. When m = 5, n = 23; m = 9, n = 35. n partial as m. Find n when m = 12.
Step 1: 23 = a + 5k
Step 2: 35 = a + 9k
Step 3: Subtract → 12 = 4k → k = 3
Step 4: 23 = a + 15 → a = 8
Step 5: n = 8 + 3×12 = 44
Answer: 44
10. Cost C = fixed + variable per km. C = ₦500 for 100 km, ₦800 for 200 km. Find C for 300 km.
Step 1: 500 = a + 100k
Step 2: 800 = a + 200k
Step 3: Subtract → 300 = 100k → k = 3
Step 4: 500 = a + 300 → a = 200
Step 5: C = 200 + 3×300 = ₦1,100
Answer: ₦1,100

(e) Percentage Increase and Decrease in Variation

Very easy explanation

If y ∝ xⁿ, then new y / old y = (new x / old x)ⁿ
% change = [(new/old)ⁿ − 1] × 100%

10 Full Examples with Step-by-Step

1. y ∝ x. x increases by 20%. Find % increase in y.
Step 1: new x = 1.2 × old x
Step 2: new y = 1.2 × old y
Step 3: % change = (1.2 – 1) × 100% = 20%
Answer: 20% increase
2. y ∝ x². x increases by 10%. Find % increase in y.
Step 1: new x = 1.1 × old x
Step 2: new y / old y = (1.1)² = 1.21
Step 3: % change = (1.21 – 1) × 100% = 21%
Answer: 21% increase
3. y ∝ 1/x. x increases by 25%. Find % change in y.
Step 1: new x = 1.25 × old x
Step 2: new y / old y = 1 / 1.25 = 0.8
Step 3: % change = (0.8 – 1) × 100% = –20% → 20% decrease
Answer: 20% decrease
4. z ∝ √x. x decreases by 36%. Find % change in z.
Step 1: new x = 0.64 × old x
Step 2: new z / old z = √0.64 = 0.8
Step 3: % change = (0.8 – 1) × 100% = –20% → 20% decrease
Answer: 20% decrease
5. P varies jointly as Q and R. Q increases 30%, R decreases 20%. Find % change in P.
Step 1: new P / old P = 1.3 × 0.8 = 1.04
Step 2: % change = (1.04 – 1) × 100% = 4%
Answer: 4% increase
6. y ∝ x³. x increases by 50%. Find % increase in y.
Step 1: new x = 1.5 × old x
Step 2: new y / old y = (1.5)³ = 3.375
Step 3: % change = (3.375 – 1) × 100% = 237.5%
Answer: 237.5% increase
7. Cost C ∝ n². n increases by 15%. Find % increase in C.
Step 1: new n = 1.15 × old n
Step 2: new C / old C = (1.15)² = 1.3225
Step 3: % change = 32.25%
Answer: 32.25% increase
8. Time t ∝ 1/speed. Speed increases by 25%. Find % decrease in time.
Step 1: new speed = 1.25 × old speed
Step 2: new t / old t = 1 / 1.25 = 0.8
Step 3: % change = –20% → 20% decrease
Answer: 20% decrease
9. Volume V ∝ r² h. r increases 10%, h decreases 10%. Find % change in V.
Step 1: new V / old V = (1.1)² × 0.9 = 1.21 × 0.9 = 1.089
Step 2: % change = 8.9%
Answer: 8.9% increase
10. y ∝ x⁴. x decreases by 50%. Find % decrease in y.
Step 1: new x = 0.5 × old x
Step 2: new y / old y = (0.5)⁴ = 0.0625
Step 3: % change = (0.0625 – 1) × 100% = –93.75% → 93.75% decrease
Answer: 93.75% decrease
