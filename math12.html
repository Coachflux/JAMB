1️⃣ PLANE FIGURES (2D SHAPES)

Rectangles & Squares 📐

🔢 Example 1: Rectangle

A rectangular field is 80m long and 60m wide. Find area and perimeter.

Area: A = l × b = 80 × 60 = 4800 m²

Perimeter: P = 2(80 + 60) = 2(140) = 280 m

🔢 Example 2: Square from Diagonal

The diagonal of a square is 10√2 cm. Find its area.

For square: diagonal = a√2
Therefore: a√2 = 10√2 → a = 10 cm

Area = a² = 100 cm²

Triangles 🔺

Standard: A = 12 × base × height
Heron's: A = √[s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)] where s = a+b+c2

🔢 Example 3: Heron's Formula

Find area of triangle with sides 13cm, 14cm, 15cm.

s = 13+14+152 = 21

A = √[21(21-13)(21-14)(21-15)]
A = √[21 × 8 × 7 × 6]
A = √7056 = 84 cm²

🔢 Example 4: Basic Triangle

Base 12cm, height 8cm. Find area.

A = 12 × 12 × 8 = 48 cm²

Quadrilaterals 🛑

🔢 Example 5: Rhombus

Diagonals 16cm and 12cm. Find area.

A = 12 × 16 × 12 = 96 cm²

🔢 Example 6: Trapezium

Parallel sides 10cm and 6cm, height 4cm. Find area.

A = 12(10 + 6) × 4
A = 12 × 16 × 4 = 32 cm²
2️⃣ CIRCLES (ARCS, CHORDS, SECTORS)
💡 Key Definitions: Arc = part of circumference | Chord = straight line between two points | Sector = pizza slice | Segment = chord + arc area

Arc Length & Sector Area ⭕

🔢 Example 7: Arc Length

Find arc length subtending 60° at center, radius 21cm. (π = 227)

Arc = θ360° × 2πr
= 60360 × 2 × 227 × 21
= 16 × 132 = 22 cm

🔢 Example 8: Sector Area

Sector radius 14cm, angle 90°. Find area.

A = 90360 × 227 × 14²
= 14 × 227 × 196
= 14 × 616 = 154 cm²

Chord Length & Segment Area 🎵

🔢 Example 9: Chord Length

Chord subtends 120° at center, radius 10cm.

c = 2r sin(θ2)
= 2 × 10 × sin(60°)
= 20 × √32 = 10√3 ≈ 17.32 cm

🔢 Example 10: Segment Area

Minor segment: chord subtends 60°, radius 42cm.

Sector Area:
= 60360 × 227 × 42²
= 16 × 227 × 1764 = 924 cm²

Triangle Area:
= 12 × 42² × sin(60°)
= 12 × 1764 × √32 ≈ 763.83 cm²

Segment: 924 - 763.83 = 160.17 cm²
3️⃣ SOLID GEOMETRY (3D SHAPES)

Cuboids & Cylinders 🥫

🔢 Example 11: Cuboid

Dimensions 8cm × 6cm × 4cm. Find volume and surface area.

Volume: 8 × 6 × 4 = 192 cm³

Surface Area:
= 2(8×6 + 6×4 + 8×4)
= 2(48 + 24 + 32)
= 2(104) = 208 cm²

🔢 Example 12: Cylinder

Radius 7cm, height 10cm. Find volume and total surface area.

Volume:
= 227 × 7² × 10
= 227 × 49 × 10 = 1540 cm³

Curved SA:
= 2 × 227 × 7 × 10 = 440 cm²

Total SA:
= 2 × 227 × 7 × (7 + 10)
= 44 × 17 = 748 cm²

Cones & Spheres 🔮

🔢 Example 13: Cone

Base radius 6cm, height 8cm. Find volume and curved SA.

Slant height:
l = √(r² + h²) = √(36 + 64) = √100 = 10 cm

Volume:
= 13 × 227 × 36 × 8
= 633621 ≈ 301.71 cm³

Curved SA:
= 227 × 6 × 10
= 13207 ≈ 188.57 cm²

🔢 Example 14: Sphere

Radius 21cm. Find volume and surface area.

Volume:
= 43 × 227 × 21³
= 43 × 227 × 9261 = 38808 cm³

Surface Area:
= 4 × 227 × 441 = 5544 cm²

Composite Figures 🧩

🔢 Example 15: Cylinder + Hemisphere

Cylinder (r=7cm, h=10cm) with hemisphere on top. Find total SA.

Cylinder (curved):
= 2 × 227 × 7 × 10 = 440 cm²

Base:
= 227 × 49 = 154 cm²

Hemisphere (curved):
= 2 × 227 × 49 = 308 cm²

Total: 440 + 154 + 308 = 902 cm²
4️⃣ EARTH AS A SPHERE

⚠️ Key Constants

Radius of Earth R = 6400 km (unless stated otherwise)
Great Circle: center at Earth's center
Longitude: 0° to 180°E/W | Latitude: 0° to 90°N/S

Longitudes & Latitudes 🌍

Along Great Circle: D = θ360° × 2πR
Along Parallel: D = θ360° × 2πR cos φ
Radius of Parallel: r = R cos φ

🔢 Example 16: Same Longitude

Towns A (30°N) and B (20°S) on same longitude. Find distance. (R=6400km)

Angular difference = 30° + 20° = 50°

D = 50360 × 2 × 227 × 6400
= 50360 × 2816007
= 140800002520 ≈ 5587.3 km

🔢 Example 17: Same Latitude

Points P and Q on 60°N, longitudes 20°E and 40°E. Find distance.

Longitude difference = 40° - 20° = 20°
Radius of parallel = 6400 × cos(60°) = 6400 × 0.5 = 3200 km

D = 20360 × 2 × 227 × 3200
= 118 × 1408007
= 140800126 ≈ 1117.46 km

🔢 Example 18: Shortest Distance

Two points on equator separated by 90° longitude.

D = 90360 × 2 × 227 × 6400
= 14 × 2816007
= 28160028 ≈ 10057.14 km
📝 EXAM TIPS & PRACTICE

🎯 JAMB Exam Strategy

• Always draw diagrams when not provided

• π = 22/7 when radius divisible by 7; otherwise π = 3.142

• Check units - convert all to same unit before calculating

• Composite figures: Break into simple shapes

• Earth calculations: R = 6400 km default

⚡ Quick Reference Values

√2 ≈ 1.414 | √3 ≈ 1.732

sin(30°) = 0.5 | cos(30°) = √32

sin(45°) = cos(45°) = √22

sin(60°) = √32 | cos(60°) = 0.5

Practice Problems:

Answer: 90°

Q2: Cone volume 1232 cm³, base radius 7cm. Find height.

Answer: 24 cm

Answer: ≈ 3351 km

Q4: Tank 2m × 1.5m × 1m full of water. How many liters? (1m³ = 1000L)

Answer: 3000 liters
