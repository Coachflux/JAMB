🦴 Support & Movement

JAMB Chapter 9: Skeleton, Plants & Movement

🧠 1. Why Support & Movement?

🔹 Support

  • Maintains body shape
  • Keeps organism upright
  • Prevents collapse

🔹 Movement

  • Search for food
  • Escape predators
  • Respond to stimuli
🧠 MEMORY TIP:

No support + no movement = No survival!

🌿 2. Movement in Plants

🌞 A. Tropic Movements

Plant bending towards light (Phototropism)

Type Stimulus Response
Phototropism Light Shoot grows toward light
Geotropism Gravity Root down, shoot up
Hydrotropism Water Roots grow toward water
Thigmotropism Touch Climbers wrap around support

⚡ B. Tactic Movement

Movement of whole organism → Example: Euglena moving to light

🌸 C. Nastic Movement

Not directional → Example: Touch-me-not closing

🌙 D. Sleep Movement

Leaves fold at night (Nyctinasty)

Auxins: Plant hormones that control bending and growth

🌱 3. Supporting Tissues in Plants

Tissue Feature Function
Collenchyma Living, flexible Support young plants
Sclerenchyma Dead, thick Strong support
Xylem Water vessels Support + transport
Phloem fibres Tough fibres Strength

🦴 4. Supporting Tissues in Animals

Human skeleton showing bones

  • Bone: Hard, contains calcium
  • Cartilage: Flexible (nose, ear)
  • Chitin: Exoskeleton in insects

🐜 5. Types of Skeleton

Exoskeleton
  • Outside body
  • Made of chitin
  • Insects, crabs
Endoskeleton
  • Inside body
  • Bone + cartilage
  • Humans, mammals

🦴 6. Mammalian Skeleton

🧠 Axial Skeleton

Vertebral Column

Region Number Function
Cervical 7 Supports head, allows neck movement
Thoracic 12 Attach ribs, protect heart & lungs
Lumbar 5 Supports body weight
Sacral 5 (fused) Connects spine to pelvis
Caudal 4 Balance (tail region)
🧠 MEMORY TRICK:

"Breakfast At 7, Lunch At 12, Dinner At 5" → (C7, T12, L5)

🦾 Appendicular Skeleton

Includes: Arms, legs, shoulder & hip

🦴 7. Functions of Skeleton

  • Support body
  • Protection (brain, heart, lungs)
  • Movement with muscles
  • Helps breathing

🔗 8. Types of Joints

Joint Movement Example
Ball & Socket All directions Shoulder, Hip
Hinge One direction Elbow, Knee
Pivot Rotation Neck
Gliding Sliding Wrist, Ankle

🔥 JAMB HOT POINTS

  • Vertebral column = 33 bones
  • Femur = longest bone
  • Auxin controls tropism
  • Exoskeleton = chitin
  • Endoskeleton = bone
