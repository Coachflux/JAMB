Composition of the Solar System

The solar system is the family of the Sun. It is made up of:

  • The Sun (a star that provides light and heat – it contains about 99.8% of the total mass)
  • Planets (8 major ones)
  • Natural satellites (moons)
  • Other bodies like asteroids, comets, meteoroids, and dwarf planets (e.g. Pluto)

In simple terms: Sun + everything that orbits the Sun.

Step 1: List the Planets

There are 8 planets (Pluto is now classified as a dwarf planet since 2006, so JAMB expects only 8).

In order from the Sun:

  • Mercury
  • Venus
  • Earth
  • Mars
  • Jupiter (largest planet)
  • Saturn (has beautiful rings)
  • Uranus
  • Neptune

Quick facts for JAMB:

  • Inner planets (Mercury to Mars) = rocky/terrestrial
  • Outer planets (Jupiter to Neptune) = gas/ice giants

Mnemonic to remember the order: My Very Excellent Mother Just Served Us Nachos

Step 2: Segments (Layers) of the Earth

The Earth is divided into 3 main segments (also called spheres or domains).

Segment Description Composition
Atmosphere The thin layer of air/gas surrounding the Earth Mostly nitrogen (78%) and oxygen (21%)
Lithosphere The solid, rocky part of the Earth Rocks, soil, and minerals (crust + upper mantle)
Hydrosphere All the water on Earth Oceans, seas, rivers, lakes, ice, groundwater (about 71% of surface)

Easy way to remember: Atmosphere = Air/Gas, Lithosphere = Land/Rock, Hydrosphere = Water.

Step 3: Natural Satellite of the Earth

The Earth has only one natural satellite – the Moon.

(It orbits the Earth once every 27.3 days.)

Step 5: The Smallest Planet that is also the Farthest from the Sun

Important JAMB note: There is no single planet that is both the smallest and the farthest from the Sun.

  • Smallest planet = Mercury (also the closest to the Sun)
  • Farthest planet = Neptune

(Older textbooks sometimes mentioned Pluto as the smallest and farthest, but since 2006 Pluto is a dwarf planet, and JAMB now expects the modern 8-planet system.)

Quick Revision Checklist

  • Solar system = Sun + planets + moons + asteroids + comets
  • Planets (8): Mercury → Neptune (use the mnemonic!)
  • Earth segments: Atmosphere (air), Lithosphere (rock), Hydrosphere (water)
  • Natural satellite: Moon
  • Smallest planet: Mercury
  • Farthest planet: Neptune
