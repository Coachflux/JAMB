Permutation and Combination: Super Easy Guide for JAMB

I will teach this topic in very simple English with everyday examples, formulas, and clear steps. Then I will give exactly 10 JAMB-style examples for (a) and 10 for (b), each with full solution and the correct option.

Quick Basics (Understand in 2 minutes)

Permutation (P) = Arrangement → Order matters.
Example: Who sits on seat 1, seat 2, seat 3? Different order = different arrangement.
Formula: ⁿPᵣ = n! / (n-r)! or simply n × (n-1) × … × (n-r+1)

Combination (C) = Selection → Order does NOT matter.
Example: Choosing 3 friends to form a group (ABC is same as BCA).
Formula: ⁿCᵣ = n! / [r! (n-r)!]

Factorial (!) reminder

  • 3! = 3×2×1 = 6
  • 4! = 24
  • 5! = 120
  • 6! = 720
  • 7! = 5040
  • 8! = 40320
  • 9! = 362880
  • 10! = 3,628,800

Now let’s go to the exact syllabus parts.

(a) Linear and Circular Arrangements

Linear arrangement = Straight line or row (order matters, positions are different).
n distinct objects → n! ways
Selecting & arranging r out of n → ⁿPᵣ

Circular arrangement = Round table or circle.
Rotations look the same (turning the table does not create a new arrangement).
So we fix one person’s position and arrange the rest → (n-1)! ways.

(The pictures above show why circular uses (n-1)! — the round table makes rotations identical.)

10 JAMB-Style Examples for (a) Linear and Circular Arrangements

1. In how many ways can 9 people be seated if only 3 chairs are available? (JAMB 2009 style)
Step 1: Linear arrangement (row of chairs).
Step 2: Select and arrange 3 out of 9.
Step 3: ⁹P₃ = 9! / (9-3)! = 9 × 8 × 7 = 504.
Answer: 504
2. Six students are to sit in a row for a photograph. In how many ways can they be arranged?
Step 1: Linear arrangement.
Step 2: All 6 distinct students.
Step 3: 6! = 720.
Answer: 720
3. In how many ways can 4 different books be arranged on a shelf?
Step 1: Linear arrangement.
Step 2: 4 distinct books.
Step 3: 4! = 24.
Answer: 24
4. In how many ways can the letters of the word “JAMB” be arranged in a line?
Step 1: Linear arrangement.
Step 2: 4 distinct letters (J,A,M,B).
Step 3: 4! = 24.
Answer: 24
5. From 10 students, 5 are to be chosen and arranged in a line for a debate. How many ways?
Step 1: Linear arrangement.
Step 2: Select and arrange 5 out of 10.
Step 3: ¹⁰P₅ = 10 × 9 × 8 × 7 × 6 = 30,240.
Answer: 30,240
6. In how many ways can 6 people sit around a circular table? (JAMB 2024 real question)
Step 1: Circular arrangement.
Step 2: Fix one position to account for rotations.
Step 3: (6-1)! = 5! = 120.
Answer: 120
7. Five friends are sitting around a round table. In how many ways can they be seated?
Step 1: Circular arrangement.
Step 2: (5-1)! = 4! = 24.
Answer: 24
8. In how many ways can 7 beads be arranged on a circular necklace (rotations same)?
Step 1: Circular arrangement.
Step 2: (7-1)! = 6! = 720.
(Note: JAMB usually does not divide by 2 unless flipping is mentioned.)
Answer: 720
9. Four couples (8 people) sit around a circular table. In how many ways if rotations are considered the same?
Step 1: Circular arrangement.
Step 2: (8-1)! = 7! = 5040.
Answer: 5040
10. How many different circular arrangements are possible for 3 distinct keys on a key ring?
Step 1: Circular arrangement.
Step 2: (3-1)! = 2! = 2.
Answer: 2

(b) Arrangements Involving Repeated Objects

When some objects/letters are identical, we divide by the factorial of the number of repeats to avoid counting identical arrangements multiple times.
Formula for distinct arrangements of n objects where some are repeated:
n! / (k₁! × k₂! × … × kₘ!)
where k₁, k₂ … are the frequencies of each repeated item.

10 JAMB-Style Examples for (b) Arrangements with Repeated Objects

1. In how many ways can the letters of the word ACCEPTANCE be arranged? (JAMB 2008 real)
Step 1: 10 letters: A×2, C×3, E×2, N×1, P×1, T×1.
Step 2: Total arrangements = 10! / (2! × 3! × 2!) = 3628800 / (2 × 6 × 2) = 3628800 / 24 = 151200.
Answer: 151200
2. In how many ways can the letters of the word ZOOLOGY be arranged? (JAMB 2022 real)
Step 1: 7 letters: Z×1, O×3, L×1, G×1, Y×1.
Step 2: 7! / 3! = 5040 / 6 = 840.
Answer: 840
3. How many different 8-letter words are possible using the letters of the word SYLLABUS? (JAMB 2023 real)
Step 1: 8 letters: S×2, Y×1, L×2, A×1, B×1, U×1.
Step 2: 8! / (2! × 2!) = 40320 / 4 = 10080.
Answer: 10080
4. Find the number of permutations of the letters of the word SCHOOL. (JAMB 2025 real)
Step 1: 6 letters: S×1, C×1, H×1, O×2, L×1.
Step 2: 6! / 2! = 720 / 2 = 360.
Answer: 360
5. The word HANDIER can be arranged in how many distinct ways? (JAMB 2025 real)
Step 1: 7 letters: H×1, A×1, N×1, D×1, I×1, E×1, R×1 (all distinct).
Step 2: 7! = 5040.
Answer: 5040
6. In how many ways can the letters of MATHEMATICS be arranged?
Step 1: 11 letters: M×2, A×2, T×2, H×1, E×1, I×1, C×1, S×1.
Step 2: 11! / (2! × 2! × 2!) = 39916800 / 8 = 4989600.
Answer: 4989600
7. How many distinct arrangements of the letters in MISSISSIPPI?
Step 1: 11 letters: M×1, I×4, S×4, P×2.
Step 2: 11! / (4! × 4! × 2!) = 39916800 / (24 × 24 × 2) = 39916800 / 1152 = 34650.
Answer: 34650
8. The letters of the word BOOK are arranged. How many distinct words?
Step 1: 4 letters: B×1, O×2, K×1.
Step 2: 4! / 2! = 24 / 2 = 12.
Answer: 12
9. In how many ways can the letters of COMMITTEE be arranged?
Step 1: 9 letters: C×1, O×1, M×2, I×1, T×2, E×2.
Step 2: 9! / (2! × 2! × 2!) = 362880 / 8 = 45360.
Answer: 45360
10. How many different arrangements can be made with the letters of the word BANANA?
Step 1: 6 letters: B×1, A×3, N×2.
Step 2: 6! / (3! × 2!) = 720 / (6 × 2) = 720 / 12 = 60.
Answer: 60

Final Tips for JAMB

  • Read the question carefully: “arranged in a line/row” → linear (n!).
  • “around a table / circular” → (n-1)!.
  • Repeated letters → always divide by the factorials of repeats.
  • If it says “select” without order → use Combination.
  • Practice calculating factorials fast (or use calculator in CBT).
