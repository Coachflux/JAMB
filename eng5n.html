🧠 One-line recall

“MD discovers hidden staff parking → suspects theft → cooperative loan truth → board warns with proverb → new loan cap.”

1️⃣ Setting: Long Break at Stardom

It is long break at Stardom Schools in Lekki, Lagos. Students are everywhere—playing and moving through corridors and the premises.

2️⃣ MD’s Walk (What Triggers the Incident)

The Managing Director, Mrs. Ibidun Gloss, has lived with a painful chronic medical condition for over 30 years. She usually goes to her private rest room during breaks, but this time she decides to walk.

3️⃣ The Discovery: Secret Parking on Expansion Land

Mrs. Gloss walks to a fenced land the school acquired two years earlier for future expansion (about five minutes from the back gate).

What shocks her

  • Teachers and staff are using it as an unofficial parking lot
  • About 17 vehicles (cars and buses) are parked there secretly

Meaning: staff hide their cars so management won’t see what they own.

4️⃣ Jombo the Security Guard Explains

The security guard, Jombo, explains that teachers park there to conceal their vehicles from management/owners.

5️⃣ Suspicion & Investigation

The MD is shocked by the quality of the cars and wonders how staff can afford them on school salaries. She suspects possible financial mismanagement or theft.

  • She summons Mr. Bepo Adewale (Principal)
  • And Mr. Jeremi Amos (Accountant)

6️⃣ The Truth: Stardom Cooperative Society

Bepo and Jeremi Amos reassure her that school finances are intact and there is no stealing.

Most vehicles were bought using loans from the Stardom Cooperative Society.

Cooperative figures (memorize)

  • Total funds: about ₦95 million
  • Loans already given: over ₦50 million
🧠 What worries the MD

Too much borrowing can become a future disaster—even if nobody is stealing now.

7️⃣ Emergency Board Meeting & The Proverb

An emergency board meeting is called. The board chairman, Chief Mrs. Solape Bayo (MD’s mother), addresses the issue.

“It’s like hanging a snake in the roof and going to bed.”
— Chief Mrs. Solape Bayo (Board Chairman)

Meaning: ignoring a hidden danger (like huge loans/indiscipline) will cause trouble later.

8️⃣ Outcome: New Rules (Loan Cap)

The board introduces new rules, including capping loan amounts from the cooperative to encourage financial prudence and reduce risk.
